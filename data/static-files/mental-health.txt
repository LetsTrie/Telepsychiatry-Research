Everyone has an interest to learn about his/her mental health, personality,
intelligence and capabilities. The assessment can be completed by yourself or
with the help our psychologist. It can be very short and sometimes it takes more
than an hour to complete. You can select which type of assessment you are
interested and its details before initiating the test. Most of the assessment has
been validated in our cultural context and can be completed in Bangla.
Depression Assessment, IQ test, Personality assessment are few popular tests
that our user usually preferred. All the assessment are done considering the
mental wellbeing of clients.
